PROJECT SHOWCASE

WHATS THIS FOR?

Welcome to the PesterFest Project Showcase! This is a spotlight segment for cool work made by the community!

If you’ve made something you’re proud of (finished or still in progress), this is your chance to share it in a way that feels organized and easy for attendees to discover.

IMPORTANT LIMITS (PLEASE READ)

  • At most, 30 total projects will be accepted.
  • We will close this form on March 20th OR once we get 100 responses.

We’re keeping the lineup tight so every accepted project actually gets seen. If the form closes early, that’s why.

HOW IT WORKS

    The gist: you submit a project, we review it, and then we feature accepted projects during the convention in a dedicated showcase space.

  • 1) Submit your project: title, short description, credits, and a link or media.
  • 2) We review + build the showcase: we’ll confirm you’re in (or follow up with questions if needed).
  • 3) You get featured: accepted projects appear in the showcase with clear info so attendees can actually engage.

Keep in mind: A clear description goes a long way. Tell people what it is, who it’s for, and what you want them to do next.

WHAT YOU CAN SUBMIT

We’re open to a wide range of projects—as long as it’s something you made (or helped make) and you’re comfortable sharing it publicly.

  • Show off your comics, animations, music, etc.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Be appropriate. Keep submissions appropriate for a broad audience and public viewing.
  • Credit matters. List collaborators and include any relevant permissions/attribution.
  • Link responsibly. If you’re linking to downloads or external pages, make sure they’re safe to click.
  • READ THE AGREEMENT FORM
