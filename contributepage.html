WHATS THIS FOR?

Welcome to the behind-the-scenes crew for PesterFest! If you’ve ever watched an online event and thought, “I want to help make that,” this is your sign.

We’re building a convention that feels fun and welcoming. That takes people who can jump in, communicate, and follow through whether you’re here for one small task or you want to be part of the core team.

HOW IT WORKS

    The gist: you pick a role, we get you set up, and you help make the convention better for everyone.

  • 1) Pick your lane: moderation, etc, etc
  • 2) Get on board: we’ll share tools and a clear first task so you’re not guessing what to do.
  • 3) Participate: check in, ask questions early, and ship small improvements that add up fast.

Pro-tip: Tell us what you actually enjoy doing. “I’m good at moderating servers” and “I love making checklists” are both extremely valuable skills around here.

WHERE WE NEED HELP

Pick what sounds like you, don't be shy!

  • COMMUNITY + MODERATION — Keep spaces welcoming, answer questions, and help folks feel included.
  • PROGRAMMING + SCHEDULING — Organize panels, workshops, premieres, and meetups so things run clean.
  • TECH/PANEL SUPPORT — Help with setup, troubleshooting, and “we go live in two minutes” moments.
  • GUEST + CREATOR SUPPORT — Coordinate with speakers, artists, and vendors so they feel prepared and appreciated.
  • WRITING + INFO — Announcements, guides, FAQs, schedule blurbs—make it easy to know what’s happening.
  • DESIGN + SOCIAL — Graphics, promos, templates, and posts that make the convention feel cohesive.

EXPECTATIONS (KEEP IT SIMPLE)

  • Be kind, be clear. We’re all working together. Assume good intent, ask questions early, and keep it constructive.
  • Do what you claim. If you take a task, follow through—or tell us quickly if life happens.
  • Keep it PG-13. Public-facing spaces should be friendly and appropriate for a wide audience.
  • We’re here to make it fun. Take the work seriously enough that the event feels effortless.
